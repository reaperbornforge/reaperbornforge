Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Head Volley Clash, we may collect the following data:

  • Account Information: nickname, game progress, owned skins, in-game currency (coins, gems), and energy level
  • Gameplay Metrics: match results, win/loss history, challenge progression, level milestones
  • Device Information: operating system, screen size, device model, and game version
  • Ad Interactions: data regarding rewarded ads viewed voluntarily

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To save and restore your game progress using secure cloud storage (Firebase)
  • To improve game experience and balance using anonymous gameplay metrics
  • To deliver optional rewarded ads via Unity Ads
  • To enable in-app purchases via Google Play Billing

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following trusted platforms:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, cloud storage, and database
  • Unity Ads: delivery of rewarded video advertisements
  • Google Play Billing: processing of purchases made within the app

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain user data for as long as the account remains active. Data is encrypted and securely stored on Firebase servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data associated with your account
  • Request deletion of your game data
  • Withdraw consent for specific features (such as ads)

To exercise these rights, please email us at: contact@reaperbornforge.com
